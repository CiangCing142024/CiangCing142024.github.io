19/03/2024

According to the newspaper The direct , an operation of the Mossos d’Esquadra has arrested 9 people in the metropolitan area of Barcelona for solidarity actions with the heroic Palestinian national resistance on February 7, when a great mass mobilization in the city was held.

The detainees are accused of crimes of public disorders, damage, illicit demonstration and criminal group, all of them crimes that can end prison sentences. At the moments that this note is written (the afternoon of Tuesday, March 19), the detainees remain in police custody at the Barcelona Cortes police station.
