On March 15, activists of the New Democracy - Manaus support committee, held a victorious brigade, during which they sold the Edition 254 of the and.

During the speeches, the activists highlighted not only the defense of the heroic Palestinian National Resistance, but also the role of accomplice that the press monopoly has in the ongoing genocide.

The activists were warmly welcomed by the masses, who listened enthusiastically to their explanations. The students who could not afford to buy the edition at the time photographed the cover of the newspaper so that they could look it up on the internet later.
