The German state continues its repressive push towards the Pro-Palestinian movements. MP Kerstin Köditz (Die Linke) has Thus recently requested the prohibition of the association "Handala Leipzig ”. This anti -colonial association, made up of members of the Palestinian diaspora and solidarity people, in fact organized many actions since the resistance counter-offensive Palestinian of October 7. In January, the Swedish activist for the Climate Greta Thunberg had notably joined an action by the group.
