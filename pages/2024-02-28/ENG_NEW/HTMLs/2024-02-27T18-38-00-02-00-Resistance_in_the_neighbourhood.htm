The class course calls workers and the people of Patras to strike rallies On Wednesday, February 28 at 10:30 at the Labor Center with a concentration at 10:00 by the Appendix.

The struggles must be escalated with a common front of workers-agents against the barbaric policies of poverty, exploitation and class barriers!

To claim:

Collective contracts with real increases in wages that cover the cost of living.

Human working conditions, labor and trade union rights, free and independent trade unionism.

Free training and care for everyone, without conditions.
