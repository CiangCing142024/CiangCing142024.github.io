The class of Ioannina calls on all workers on strike, tomorrow on February 28 and at the strike at 10:30 at the Labor Center.

We are fighting for:
  • Increases in wages, covering the cost of living
  • human conditions
  • free and independent trade unionism by the state
  • Overturn of Chatzidakis Laws - Georgiadis
  • Free training and care for everyone, without conditions.
