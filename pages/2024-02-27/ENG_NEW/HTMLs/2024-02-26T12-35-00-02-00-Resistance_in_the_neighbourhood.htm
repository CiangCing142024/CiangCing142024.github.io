Front of Resistance and RACE

AGAINST ANTI-LAW-ANTILAKI POLICY

ALL IN STRUGGLE 28 VERVARI

Concentration in Havtia 11am

For weeks, youth and farmers have been on the road and stand against the seizure attack that has launched the system against their rights.

Students and students with mass assemblies, occupations and demonstrations clearly state that they will not accept the black future they are planning for government and system. They defend public free education against a few and fine education! At the same time, farmers are massively involved in the blocks against the anti -grocery policy and the EU, against the new joint agricultural policy claiming compensation for 100% of the losses and increases in incomes that cover the cost of living.

At the same time that these mass matches have erupted, the working class, The workers and the people are increasingly immersed in poverty and poverty with precision crushing popular income. The government knows very well that society is a boiling cauldron, so it is trying to mislead us with communication tricks such as the upcoming supposed increase in minimum wage. It is constantly equipped with new anti-labor and anti-populist bills, culminating in the laws of Hatzidakis and Georgiadis, which, among other things, abolish the 5-day-8-hour period and hit the right to strike and class trade unionism. Tempi's crime revealed once again that our lives are sacrificed on the altar of profit. A year after the prescribed crime and the mass strikes that broke out shortly thereafter, the system continues to prove to be indifferent to human life.

It is now clear that all that this system has to do with is cutting rights, poverty and a medieval work. It won't pass them! It is imperative for workers to go out on the road, to claim wages, work and life with rights. Against the silent fish that trade unions are trying to impose, culminating in the attitude of the GSEE, which announced a strike on April 17! To massive the upcoming strike, alongside the youth and the farmers who are already struggling, on a front -system resistance front! Only the massive and relentless struggle of people and youth can put a brake on anti -populist and anti -labor policy!
