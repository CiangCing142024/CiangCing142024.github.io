Presidium at the Turin Court, Corso Vittorio Emanuele 130, 9.00 - 13.00.
For Roberto, Marco, Filippo
on 18 December 2021 in Turin, in via Genova, for the collapse of the crane
We are aware of the importance and need of the solidarity that is given and received: we express it to the family members of victims, friends, colleagues.
Solidarity breaks isolation, it is aware of the huge tragedy e of the pain that derives from any death at work, for work, from Work.
We know of the thread that binds this tragedy to that of Florence of February 16, with 5 dead workers (Luigi, Mohamed, Bouzekri, Mohamed, Taoufik), also in a construction site; to that of Brandizzo (TO), of 30 August '23, where 5 contract workers lost their lives (Giuseppe, Kevin, Giuseppe, Michael, Giuseppe), torn by a train in a construction site of the Italian railway network (RFI); to that of 5-6 December over 16 years ago at Thyssenkrupp, always in Turin, with 7 Live burnt workers (Giuseppe, Rosario, Angelo, Bruno, Antonio, Roberto, Rocco).
The same thread that binds disappeared workers and workers in anonymity, at most remember only one day or reported in a Trafiletto.
In the first month and a half of 2024, in Piedmont, they lost their lives 7 workers, including: Alessandro, while he was driving the bus; Mario, for an illness at Rogelfrut; Domenico, in the clash with a vehicle; Mohamed, Tunisian electrician; Giovanni, who fell from 27 meters into Assembly of a crane in Rivoli.
Just three months ago, on November 27, in Caselette (to) they precipitated, For the umpteenth collapse of a crane, two workers, seriously injuring.
The construction sites is the sector where you die more, but no place is sure!
In 2023 there were 1,485 fatal accidents at work and in itinere, more than one every 8 hours; 583,356 the complaints of accident, of which many disabled; 72,754 occupational diseases, increased by 20% Compared to 2022. From the beginning of 2024, 197 victims are at work and in itinere, 4 per day.
Those who are no longer leaves a difficult but necessary legacy towards them and towards workers, the workers and the community: continue and develop the struggle for safety and health every day in each place of work and on the territory.
We are here for Roberto, Marco, Filippo
and for all workers and workers who died in the name of profit
Unity is strength! The struggle makes the difference!
February 21, 2024
- Coordination 12 October
[Constituted after the day of mobilization of 12 October a Bologna. Coordination formed by family members of massacres, associations and Committees, realities and trade union activists]
