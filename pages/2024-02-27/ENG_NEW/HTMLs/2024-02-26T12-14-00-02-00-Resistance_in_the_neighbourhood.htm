THE FUTURE WE WILL WIN OUR ROADS

EVERYTHING IN STRUGGLE ON 28/2


A year after the crime in Tempi where 57 people were murdered, the system continues to prove that it does not care about our lives and our future. The people and the youth go out on the streets and question the anti -populist policies. For two months students are mobilized, With general assemblies (unprecedented mass), occupations and mass demonstrations against Pierrakakis for private universities. Farmers set up blocks against accuracy and anti -righteous policies, against the new CAP and the general government-EU attack. Teleperformance workers are mobilized, strike and claiming rights. Task-US and CERTH employees are mobilized Against the redundancies of their colleagues and employer threats. The people and the youth show that the attack on lives and rights will not go easily.

At the same time the system intensifies its attack. The anti -labor laws Hatzidakis and Georgiadis strike the rights of workers in the eight -hour, strike, trade union organization and beyond. Law 4777 and the Law Framework strike the rights of students in public-dimensional studies bring tuition, deletions and disciplinarys, the secondment of professional rights from degrees. The policy of class barriers with the EBE and the Bank of themes throws students from universities and the law on technical education throws students from schools and makes them cheap, exploitable, without rights. They have submitted the bill that allows the establishment of private universities and will directly affect the rights of those who study in public with tuition, more intensification of our terms of study, more class barriers, abolition of trade unionism and fragmentation and degradation. They want to bring the national diploma that will mean nationwide for all 3 classes of high school.

Their attack should not pass! The claims that have erupted, students, students, farmers of the rest of the people have scared the system. That is why he is recruiting repression to stop them, so students impose on the students, so he brings out the strikes, unions and collective claims in illegality.

We have to make their fears a reality. The struggles of the students, the workers, the whole people must be linked to each other. Our claims are our lives and our future. They relate to the defense of our rights, the rights of the whole people in life, studies and work with rights. Together we must fight for:

  • Increases in wages and pensions to cover the cost of living
  • Public - Free Education for all without class barriers
  • Labor rights
  • Professional Rights Degree - Unique Prerequisite for Stable Work
  • Against private universities
  • Against Pierrakakis and the Law on Vocational Education
  • Against the National Bachelor's Degree
  • Against tuition, deletions and disciplinary

Through our collective struggles, unions and student associations, our general assemblies and occupations, strikes and demonstrations we will respond to anti -populist politics. Through the collective and single struggles of people and youth, students and workers we will conquer studies - work - life with rights
