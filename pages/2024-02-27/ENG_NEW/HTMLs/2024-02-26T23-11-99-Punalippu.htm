Saturday 24.2. Hundreds of people gathered to protest against the Rafah bombing. The emblems required, among other things, an immediate ceasefire, the closure of Israel's Eurovision Song Contest, and crushing of imperialism and Zionism. The demonstration went smoothly without problems with the police.
