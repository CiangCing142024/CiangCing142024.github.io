The newspaper newspaper The New Democracy He is proud and pleased to announce that the outstanding historian, popular educator and Marxist communicator, João Carvalho, is the new member of the editorial board of AND .

João Carvalho is a graduate historian from the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG), with complementary training in classic letters and a master's degree in Social History from USP; Currently, the Doctorate in Social History of Culture from UFMG. Militant, João Carvalho dedicates continuous efforts to the diffusion of Marxism on Youtube and other platforms, from where he has achieved a great influence, and also organizes and sponsors the distribution initiatives of Marxist books to popular masses. Now, it also takes over your post as a member of the editorial board of the newspaper The New Democracy ( AND ).
