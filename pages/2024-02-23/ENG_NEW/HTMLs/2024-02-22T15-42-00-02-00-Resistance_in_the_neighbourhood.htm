The Solidarity Committee in the struggle of the Palestinian people, in the context of its actions to promote and support the heroic struggle of the Palestinian people, is organizing an event that will present poems from the Palestinian poet Essham-Theodos' collection.


Saturday 24 February at 7:30 pm at Art Cafe Suite in Trikala

Poems will be recited by Gougara Vaso and Zacharopoulos Sotiris. Latsaras Dimitris will accompany his guitar.
