Expenses in Europe

Military spending in Europe last year arrived around 345 billion dollars, according to Sipri, compared to 230 billion of 2014, the year in which the controversial was established in the NATO Summit of Newport political objective of raising the military spending of the "allies" of the States United at least to 2% of the GDP.
