With great mass, over 400 protesters, and with the participation of workers, students, students and farmers, the strike was held in Vathi, Samos. Despite all the efforts of the system, with the tool of suppression, terrorism and the exhaustion of struggles, as well as the subordinate trade union leaders, people have shown that they can and need to turn his anger and indignation into a struggle.

The gathering was attended by the ESA, the Hospital Workers' Association, the two educational associations, as well as the Bess Student Association. A common banner participated in the demonstration of the teacher movements and the class course.

In the course of the island on the streets of the world, the disruption of the world was obvious. ACC and T.P. They tried with their slogans to highlight the period in question. They denounced the crime of the system in Tempi, stressed the need for the people and youth to come to the forefront, on a joint struggle front, to claim better terms of work, free studies, political and trade union freedoms. Anti -imperialist slogans and slogans of solidarity were also shouted to the struggling Palestinian people.



The demonstration closed in the city square with the speeches of the representatives of the clubs and the participants. On the part of PAME/KKE that controls most of them, the tone of a festive closing of the games was obvious. Without any plan for the sequel, with general class crowns, with the wrong political estimates and management requests. With the anxiety of declaring the latest struggles of the last period that as hopeful and massive they were, they still have a way ahead of them to be more massive and overthrow government and system plans. After all, the closure of the rural struggles the day before, made the PAME/KKE aspirations obvious to be the 28th not a principle for the formation of a single joint front between workers, farmers and youth but the end of the races and subordination to urban legitimacy and systemic aspirations.

But the mass popular participation is. How the popular masses want and can go out on the street. How they need to walk against the policies of misery, bans and the constant deterioration of their lives. How the example of student youth and poor peasantry can well evolve into a mass frontal popular movement that will fight to put a brake on the attack.
The forces referring to the Left and the Movement, with all the forces that have, in all areas they intervene, need to fight to find the 28th. To fight so that people and youth do not accept the end of the struggles, not to pinch in the Euros elections that are ready to serve them from the right and the "left". To try to build terms of continuing racing mobilizations in the direction of conflict and clash with the government and the system.
