The unthinkable slave revolt

Those who say that Israel was aware of the plans of 7 October are reconfirming an old colonial common place according to to which the natives are too docile, too submissive, too cowardly and Too inferior to rebel against their oppressors.

Zubayr Alikhan - February 8, 2024

On 7 October 2023 the Palestinians launched the largest Decolonial operation in the history of Palestine. They saw the their chains, they demolished the cage and sautéed the walls of iron. The Palestinians took flight. They blinded the cameras, Communications and penetrated the settlements interrupted. They have paralyzed a colony of nuclear colonists and have brought to their knees The Empire. They cut the umbilical cord of inexpression, of the safety and sacrosanct domain, vital for all projects colonial.

Immediately, imperial scribes and politicians, settlers and administrators colonial, conservative and liberal - especially liberals - are put at work. Their task was twofold: to ensure that the world saw natives as wild hordes, barbarians, animals, quintessence del evil - Here the liberals quickly forgot theirs political correctness and havetened to condemn - and at the same time restore the domain.

The first stormed the world, but for anyone who was healthy to mind has always been fallacious and now it has been tirelessly and completely dispelled. The second, however, has permeated the mind of Many, even those potentially well intentioned. This restoration of the empire has manifested themselves in several ways: the Palestinians they could not have escaped alone, they would not have been able to penetrate there Iron dome, Israeli security systems are too much advanced to be overcome or unarmed - Israel “has let it they did. "

Without losing a blow, an unprecedented operation that shattered the concept of imperial invincibility has been reformulated as an imperial tool . It was part of a plan

wider general to strengthen and expand further the domain: the natives were senseless pawns provision of an omniscient colonial power and intellectually supreme. From this vein of thought - and in it - many emerged Other truths, half truths and lies that highlight and consolidate it the socket. According to some, Israel had actually supported and created Hamas. Netanyahu had planned all this to win the next Elections, support others. And my favorite - especially because it is now been enriched by the Zionist stenographers of New York Times - "Israel had always knew."

Surely, the next discovery will be that Israel knew of the flood of Al-Aqsa before the Palestinians themselves, who invented it, has it Presented in Hamas and inculcated it in the minds of the Palestinians. At the basis of all this - and to constitute the raison d'etre collective These theories - there is the unthinkability of the slave revolt (or gods natives).

The unthinkability of the slave revolt is a concept introduced by Michel-Rolph Trouillot to explain the answers, silences and the silence of the western empire on the Haitian revolution. While Trouillot uses it in relation to the 18th century imperialists, this unthinkability is applied without interruption to the present: the empire, its ideologies, its colonialism, its genocides and the His colonies are not stories to study, but material realities and violence lived. Today.

At the outbreak of the Haitian revolution, the most common reaction in France, England, Spain and the United States was disbelief. The news It was false. The facts-just like those of the Al-Aqsa flood- They were too improbable. In any case, the facts had to be false, Because blacks, like the Palestinians, were beasts without brain, wild forced to docility, lazy, disorganized and lower: they were absolutely unable to devise such an operation, much less than organize and make it.

Even if the rebels had somehow evoked the spirits and completed miracles, intellectual, military, economic superiority and Razziale dei Bianchi assured that they would be quickly tamed, chained and put again at work. The problem, however, was just that the West was reacting - to the fact that occurred - and devising explanations, while the blacks and the Palestinians implemented the unthinkable, actively neutralize the colonial forces and claimed the lands.

Slowly and reluctantly, the reality of the operations made its way. The news of ruthless black hordes that massacred the whites reached Europe, and the Zionists proclaimed the "horrors of San Domingo ". The news therefore had to be rationalized in one way Different: the obvious facts were, they are still unthinkable. Therefore, the revolution was “an unfortunate repercussion of the calculation errors of the farmers ", the flood of Al-Aqsa was the result of" A waterfall of False years duration of years ”of Israel,“ did not aim at a change revolutionary ”, did not aim at decolonization,“ it was not supported by the majority of the slave population ”, was Hamas who acted alone and the Palestinians did not support them, “it is due to external agitators, "Iran instigated," was the ... consequence of various connive conspiracy by non -slaves ”, there have been “Fugs of news within the security establishment of security Israeli". With blacks and Palestinians intellectually null, “each party has chosen his favorite enemy as the conspirator plus probable ... [and] he accused himself of being the mind behind the revolt". The British, the realists and the mules were in Haiti; in Palestine, Iran was the mind, and behind them, Russia and China.

Here, some will probably rush to throw insults or ad accuse me of naivety for neglecting the clear facts (as if I was the empire) of Zionist knowledge. I will clarify. None of this that I wrote is to say that the Zionist regime certainly does not he knew - even if my visceral instinct still hesitates to believe that he knew - but rather that the operation was unthinkable for them, that their knowledge/not knowing is irrelevant, and that the development of events and their results are not in their omnipotent hands, but in those of the Palestinians, of the owners of the earth and resistance.

It is interesting to note that, among all the work of tachigraphy imperial presented in the Times survey on the Al-Aqsa flood, There is a line that has been neglected and underestimated especially in effort to bomb the revolutionary successes of 7 October in the Kingdom of the Empirium, under siege, or rather, without memory.

It can therefore be deduced that this sentence has a potential truth:

“[Israel kept the] fatally inaccurate conviction that Hamas He did not have the ability to attack and would not have dared to do it ... [one] conviction thus rooted in the Israeli government ... to ignore the growing evidence of the opposite ".

Note the perception of Palestinian inability - and docility through fear - as well as the recognition that an operation of genre was so viscerally unthinkable that the Zionists, like the Europeans of 1792, they have disavowed their own eyes. Such is The arrogance of the empire, and here is its destruction.

Imperialism is based on the idea itself, on the centrality and supremacy of the ego on those who await the conquest and the civilization. The primarity of the empire rests on secondary of the other. Imperial superiority is based on native inferiority. Yet, paradoxically, this secondary and inferiority are indispensable to the primarity and superiority of the European and the Empire. Events such as the Haitian revolution, the attack on the Milk Bar e The flood of Al-Aqsa, invert, overturn or shatter completely These hierarchies.

Therefore, for the native, this violence is a "purifying force", frees him “from his inferiority complex, from his despair and inertia; It makes it fearless and restores self -respect. To do this, If the native frees itself from its inferiority complex, it is no longer inferior; If it is no longer lower, the colonizer is not higher; self The colonizer is not superior, then the idea of empire comes Compromise, the idols collapse and imperialism falls.

Therefore , writes Fanon, “it is precisely when he [the native] realizes his humanity that begins to sharpen the weapons with which The victory will ensure. " The function of rationalizing theories which clarify the unthinkable then becomes evident: it is the preservation of the domain, of the hierarchy, the future of the colonists, the imperial self.

The threat posed by any act of indigenous resistance in Any part of the colonized world is not simply a threat material for its direct objective or for the local lordship, but a existential scourge against empire and imperialism in his complex. In the always relevant words of Ghassan Kanafani, "Imperialism has placed its body in the world, her head in Asia Eastern, the heart in the Middle East, its arteries reach Africa and Latin America. Wherever you hit him, damn him and serve the World Revolution ".
