AND To strengthen this concept, economists argue: “Which Social Democrat does not know that according to Marx and Engels' doctrine The economic interests of the individual classes perform a function decisive in history and that, consequently , In particular, the struggle of the proletariat for his interests economic must have a primary meaning for its development of class and the emancipator struggle? ".

Lenin Reply: “This is consequently absolutely inappropriate. From the the fact that economic interests perform a decisive function not derives at all no consequence on the primary meaning of the economic struggle (= union), because the most essential "decisive" interests classes can be satisfied only with radical transformations policies in general; in particular, the fundamental economic interest of proletariat can only be satisfied through a revolution policy that replaces the dictatorship of the bourgeoisie of the proletariat ".
