The last of the League: wants to prohibit the events against Israel

Giacomo Salvini - Daily fact

The 4 November last the leader of the League Matteo Salvini organized A pro-Israel demonstration in Milan in which he called "fascists" those who take to the square against Tel Aviv after the events of the 7th October.

For this, with the excuse of fighting the acts of anti -Semitism, now the Carroccio would like to prohibit the events in which they are criticized The institutions of Israel: to ask for it is a bill Presented by the League in the Senate on January 30th and filed on Wednesday, Just the day when some demonstrators came to Naples Manganellati under the Rai headquarters while protesting to ask for peace In the Gaza Strip after the controversies for the Ghali phrases in Sanremo (“ Stop to the genocide ”).

There bill is signed by the group leader of the Maximilian League Romeo and senators Daisy Pirovano and Giorgio Maria Bergesio and has a Generic title: " Provisions for the adoption of the operational definition of anti -Semitism as well as for the fight against anti -Semitism documents ". A slender text of three articles in which the goal only seems to be to adopt the definition of formulated anti -Semitism by the plenary assembly of the international alliance for memory of the Holocaust in 2016.

By anti -Semitism, we read in the text, we mean " a determined perception of the Jews that can be expressed as hatred towards them, whose manifestations, of a verbal or physical nature, they are directed towards Jewish and non -Jewish people, their assets, the Community institutions and Jewish cult places ”.
