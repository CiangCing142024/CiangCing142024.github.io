We think that the best way to remember Concetta who left us on February 20, always concept active, stimulating in the initiatives of the revolutionary proletarian feminist movement, is to publish his intervention on the occasion of a March 8 a few years ago!

Happy March 8, Concetta!

Concetta the second from the left

While we parade imperialism on the streets, capitalism with the use of a modern patriarchism, launch its increasingly consistent attacks towards women with lucid determination, and these last years there have shown it.

It is up to us feminists, workers, unemployed, migrants to fight and be the avant -garde of the movement of women's liberation.

We must strike and invade the squares to claim denied rights.

The discrimination have not been swept away especially in the world of work where women remain an oppressed majority with disparities, injustices, harassment and violence both on jobs and in the home.

We know well that the road is uphill, but Determine as we are, combatives as we are we will certainly not give up.

The day of March 8 must not be an end in itself. We need to do more, perhaps the need to make strong actions that make our voices really heard and that give a truly revolutionary turning point.

Precarious worker concept Taranto
