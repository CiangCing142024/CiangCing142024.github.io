Description: Jennifer M.
Images: Last week, the Palestine Solidarity Linz organized another demonstration and called for an end to the attacks on the Palestinian people. Hundreds gathered and marched from the Volksgarten across the country road to the main square. Last week the Palestine Solidarity Linz Another demonstration and called for an end to the attacks on the Palestinian people. Hundreds gathered and marched from the Volksgarten across the country road to the main square.

The speakers of the demonstrations occasionally denounced the attitude of the ruling Austria, as well as the direct military participation of support from Israel by Austria and the associated breaks of neutrality.
