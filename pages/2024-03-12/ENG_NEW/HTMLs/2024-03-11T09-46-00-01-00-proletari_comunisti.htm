Solvay's heinous pervicacy resists - is willful misconduct! - To the amount of scientific evidence accumulated, including transcriptional comparative analysis Published in the magazine Toxics and with the title “Cross-Species Transcriptomics Analysis Highlights Conserved Molecular Responses to Per- and Polyfluoroalkyl Substances”, made by Scientists from the Department of Pharmacy and Biotechnology of the University of Bologna e of the University of Padua, Study that leaves no doubt about how much the different pfas molecules (4,730 molecules: the largest family of emerging pollutants) influence hormonal streets and metabolic streets.

The PFAS cause a strong regression of the metabolism and the transport of lipids and other processes related to ovarian development, the production of estrogen, ovulation and physiological functioning of the female reproductive system; All elements that explain the harmful effects of the PFAS on fertility and fetal development . Show that exposure to PFAS produces a soveregulation of the ID1 gene, involved in the development of various types of cancer , including leukemia, breast and pancreas cancer. The epidemiological data suggest that a high exposure also significantly increases the mortality of individuals suffering from malignant neoplasms of lymphatic and hematopoietic tissues , like a spleen, liver and bone marrow. The toxic effect of the PFAS on the immune system explains the weakening of immune reactions, the production of antibodies and responses to vaccinations, observed particularly in children exposed to the PFAS during the prenatal and postnatal period. The exposure also increases the concentration in the serum of inflammatory and oxidative stress markers and thus promotes the development of systemic diseases, such as the Hepatic damage and cardiovascular diseases, including atherosclerosis and thromboembolic events.

The new environmental protection directive includes the so -called " qualified crimes “, Which lead to the destruction or significant damage to an ecosystem and are therefore comparable to ECOCIDIDE : For example, the widespread pollution of air, water and soil.

For the defendants, the consequences for having committed an environmental crime can be prison penis Up to ten years if their action causes a person's death. In most cases, however, the penalty provided for crimes committed with negligence is five years , eight regarding the "qualified crimes “.

“The new directive opens a new page in the history of Europe, defining protection against those who damage ecosystems and, through them, human health. It means put an end to environmental impunity in Europe , crucial and urgent And "says Antonius Manders speaker of the directive. Of this impunity in Italy we have widely documented in the two books "Perfect crime environment" by Barbara Tartaglione and Lino Balza, preface by Giorgio Nebbia.
