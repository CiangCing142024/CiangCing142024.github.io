Student associations and colleges demonstrated on Tuesday (19/3) on the streets of Thessaloniki, expressing their solidarity with the 49 arrests of the MAT invasion of the AUTH on Saturday 16/3. The rally started at 8pm from Kamara, shortly after the end of the general meetings of the students.



The presence of the police was suffocating, with MAT squads constantly moving alongside the march and back. Similar police rule prevailed in the lunch of Student on Thursday 14/3. In the wake of government fake news for the alleged nuclear rifle in the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki and the persecutions prepared by Mitsotakis' statements for disciplinary disciplines, it is clear that the system will employ the hardening of repression to burn the students.



The racing moves have formed blocks with slogans against terrorism, in favor of acquitting students and with the aim of overthrowing Pierrakakis law and calling for mass participation of students at the rally on Thursday 21/3 at 12:00 in Kamara.
