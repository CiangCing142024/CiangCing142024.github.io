Straight acts are launched by women under Gabriela on March 1 at the beginning of women's month. They call: Livelihood, right, entitlement, non-foreign chacha and some!

In this day, members of Gabriela Youth went, with other youth groups, in Mendiola in Manila to reveal their opposition to Chacha in the Marcos regime.

There are similar protests within the University of the Philippines-Los Baños hosted by Gabriela Youth-UPLB. The group said, protests are union with the scheduled Purple Day of Action.

Attention to this, women's worker under the Women Workers United and May UNO movement, work, work, rights and end to exploitation of labor. On March 5, Gabriela-NCR also launched protests in Manila.
