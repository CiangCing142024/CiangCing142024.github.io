This is our meaning of March 8.

Let's say to the workers: be bold, proud, more determined on everything. If we women do it, if we become "dangerous", the government force, masters, state can be cracked, and then, then ... reversed with the proletarian revolution of all exploited and oppressed, in which women bring an extra gear.

They are a example for all of us.

We call you workers, who know well the suffering, to give unconditional support to Palestinian women, to the resistance of the Palestinian people, against the criminal action of Israel; Against the wars that the imperialist states, including ours, are bringing to every part of the world.

There is a link between us and the Palestinian women who must become more and more concrete and stronger: They are also the Meloni government, the owners who in this country that build weapons, accomplices and responsible for genocide in Palestine; These monsters as our lives use for their profits, their reactionary, racist, repressive plans to prevent women's freedom; They are the same who want to crush the Palestinian people. The struggle of Palestinian women is our struggle, It helps us against this putrefied system, and the struggle of us working women is the struggle of the Palestinians because it weakens the imperialist monsters.

Long live the proletarian and internationalist March 8th!
