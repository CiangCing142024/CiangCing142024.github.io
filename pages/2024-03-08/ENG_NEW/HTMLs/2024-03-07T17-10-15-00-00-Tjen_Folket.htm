By a commentator for the Earn Folket Media.

Our comrades in the match committee mobilize for March 8 markings against imperialism and patriarchy in Oslo, Bergen, Trondheim and Kristiansand.

The match committee encourages spreading time and place for attendance, and to join:

Oslo: 17:00 in Greenland square.

Bergen: 17:00 at Torgallmenningen and 18:30 at the blue stone.

Trondheim: 16:00 at the top of Nordre gate, and 17.00 on the square.

Kristiansand: 13:00 at the Women's Health House, 15.15 at the police station and 16:30 at Torvet.

In connection with March 8, the comrades have also published a statement where they write, among other things, that March 8 will be characterized by the Gaza genocide and the Palestinian people's heroic resistance struggle. The comrades end the statement like this:

"8. March is not a day to celebrate femininity or to congratulate each other on gender equality. March 8 is a fight day against imperialist war, against double suppression of the working women, to strengthen women's struggle and organization, against patriarchal culture and violence against women. Women show all over the world's heroism and strength in the fight. We know that this match will eventually win, and we will mark March 8 with enthusiasm and match glow. ”
