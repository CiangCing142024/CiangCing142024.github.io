Declaration

Solidarity with the Palestinian people!


The army of the Zionist and fascist state of Israel has more than 100 days of genocidal bombings and attacks by sea and land against the population of Gaza. According to the data provided by the Palestine Embassy in our country, there are already more than 24,000 killed and more than 60,000 injured. In the West Bank are 360 those killed and more than 4,000 the injured.

There are 10,400 children killed, 7,100 killed women, hundreds of murdered elders, 8,000 missing, including 4,000 children. Almost two million displaced, 60% of the houses were destroyed cowardly and barbarously.

They bomb and destroy hospitals, schools, mosques and all kinds of infrastructure while condemning hunger and lack of medical assistance to the entire population.

This is a true genocide, which takes as an excuse the actions of the Palestinian resistance of October 7 but that continues the infamous dispossession and apartheid of 75 years ago, and thus it is being considered increasingly more internationally.

In that sense and in an exemplary way the Government of South Africa filed a formidable complaint recently in The Hague, which we greet, as well as the gigantic demonstrations that are taking place in many countries and all the mobilizations that have been made in our country.

The Popular Unity considers the heroic and sacrificed struggle of the Palestinian people for its self -determination and national and social liberation as a fair struggle, which must receive the greatest solidarity of our people, since it faces the Zionist State that is a military power and is strongly supported politically, economically and militarily by Yankee imperialism and its European allies.

We repudiate the statements of the government of the coalition of the right and the ultra -right that Luis Lacalle Pou presides, with which he has once again joined the choir of Yankee imperialism in support of the Zionist State of Israel and also those of the sectors of the pseudoprogressism that They add to this reactionary choir.

In particular, we condemn that the Government has repeatedly voted at the UN the requirement of cessation to the fire claimed by the vast majority of the countries of the world.

We sympathized with Mrs. Ambassador of Palestine in our country that made a just claim in that sense, while the entertaining government celebrates that can sell more meat to the genocide.


Long live the heroic resistance of the brother Palestinian people

And international solidarity!
