The revolutionary movement, allies, and local and international friends celebrated the 85th birthday of Jose Maria Sison's companion on February 8. They launched various activities to exalt Ka Joma and appreciate her significant contributions to Philippine Revolution.

In Rizal, the Youth Makabayan (KM) -Rizal conducted a series of Oplan-Tikit and Oplan Pint in some of the key provinces on February 11. In a humanitarian area in Barangay San Juan, Taytay, young people attached “Wheat Paste Art ”featuring the image of Ka Joma. They also painted the walls of the young people who joined the New People's Army and participated in the people's war.

In Utrecht, The Netherlands, allied organizations of the National Democratic Front of the Philippines (NDFP) conducted a cultural program.
