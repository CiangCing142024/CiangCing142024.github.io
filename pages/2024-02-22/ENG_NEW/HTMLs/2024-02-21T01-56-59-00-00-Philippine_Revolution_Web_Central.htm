Among the military operated operations in the guerrilla front, red commanders and warriors were determined by a training in the basic political course-military course on January 20-27. Activity Active in arms with the fighters of basic political principles to harass the anti-revolutionary campaign in the Marcos regime.

As well as the training in dominance for the guerrilla frontage of ideological, political and organizational organizations in the conflict of the people's war, especially in the armed struggle, the New People's Army (NPA) -Central Negros.

"Training has been successful because of the revolutionary cooperation with warriors and commanders, and the unsteading mass support, financial and security of the unit," still statement of the unit.

Ka Monique, Red fighter said "" It's just different when you're training because you're not afraid to deal with the enemy especially in the central task of our army. " Politicians-military trainings are helpful as this to "avoid Munlun-military or militarist ... because of the military, it must be experienced in politics," Proclamation of Ka Turks and main training instructions.
