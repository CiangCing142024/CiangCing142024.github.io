New mass concentration, Analogous to the previous one, took place today in front of the central gate of the CERTH Research Center in Thessaloniki. The center, which absorbs large funds and is considered a "technological hub" (with visits by the Prime Minister), has recently fired about 15 workers (under the pretext of "non -finding project"). In fact, some are clearly revengeful, as the redundant workers were union workers.



The vast majority of the center workers are working with DPY. ("Block") , with what this entails: short -term contracts, not permissions (normal, sick and childbirth), not fixed hours and paid overtime, no dismissal compensation. Based on the "block" the administration of the Center masquerades with the misleading title "Finishing Contract". The need to claim permanent and stable work, more than obvious.

On this basis, And after the first mobilization, the SETIP, SMT-M unions and you were invited to a joint meeting held at the EKTH, with a significant turnout, and decided to have a new rally. An alien impression has caused the attitude of the "Workers' Association", which behaved as a genuine employer. More or less shook the finger to the workers and refused to call on mobilization! It is no coincidence, then, that the administration referred the 3 unions to ... SEEKETA (!) For the performance of their resolutions. Employees have eyes and see who is on their side and who with the employer ...







Today's concentration is judged successful , as in mass terms it raised the issue of redundancies and working relationships. These rallies give courage and practical solidarity to workers in the workplace, and of course send a message and push the employer. The fact that these mobilizations are taken seriously by the presence of a MAT cage in front of the gate, which is probably the only "argument" that the administration can propose against the rights ...

In the gathering they participated : SETIP, SMT-M, SEEE, Student Associations, Left and Anarchist Trade Unions. The racing initiative and the class march had a significant presence in the rally, as well as a role in organizing it at all stages of evolution.
