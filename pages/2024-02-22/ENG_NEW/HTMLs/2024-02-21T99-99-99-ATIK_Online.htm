Stuttgart | 21.02.2024 | 9 people who lost their lives as a result of a racist attack on immigrants in Hanau, Germany 4 years ago, were commemorated with an action in Stuttgart. The commemoration made by antifascist and immigrant organizations, including Atiif and YDG, began with the reading of the joint statement of institutions. In a statement, it was emphasized that Hanau massacre showed that racism has reached a more serious dimension in Germany and that this racism has become more dangerous with the racist party AFD, who has recently risen. From the moment the massacre took place, it was underlined that the racism of racism with the state revealed its relationship with the state. After the statement, the music about the Hanau massacre was listened to. Finally, after a commemoration, a spontaneous action was carried out on the streets of Stuttgart.
