Next Monday Our begins Fight Day in Defense of people's rights , which will be done in a context of greater repression and criminalization against the popular movement in Oaxaca.

The chronology of aggressions against the people so far this year is as follows:


16 from January. 70 proletarian families of the Revolution colony in the port of Salina Cruz were attacked by elements of the Municipal Police and the Navy while resisted the demolition of their homes initiated by the company in charge of the Construction of the interoceanic train.

27 from January. In means of a joint operation, elements of the state agency of Investigations, State Police and National Guard category the homes of 9 Civil-Mixtequilla resistance activists, which were arrested and transferred to the Tanivet prison; The next day the community was besieged by National Guard and Marina to avoid protests. Finally, last 2 of February a control judge imposed on the detainees the precautionary measure of prison justified preventive.

7 February. A District Judge based in Salina Cruz sentenced 46 years and 6 months of Prison to fellow David Hernández Salazar, Community Agent of Puente Wood and member of the APIIDTT organization, for opposing the dispossession of Communal lands.

13 February. Items Municipal Police and State Police with high -power and no team weapons riots were evicted to our fellow artisans and merchants from the Gurrion walker, next to the Temple of Santo Domingo, violating his Right to work and minutes signed with municipal governments and state.

18 February. Marine elements evict and stop with luxury of violence to retired railroads that manifested on the train tracks in Matías Rosemary.

29 February. The State Police install metal fences in the mouth-cales of the Zócalo de la city, providing new demonstrations. It should be noted that our day of Lucha begins on March 4 and will enter the Zocalo.
