Mirafiori, 2,200 workers at home. Here's what happens

Stop a Fiat 500E and Maserati, the layoffs for April 20 is triggered

Endless nightmare for Stellantis' workers in Mirafiori. Over 2,200 workers end up in layoffs until April . The reason? The Fiat 500E does not sell.

The communication came a little while ago, at the end of a meeting between the management and trade union organizations. At the moment, in Mirafiori there was the rotation layoffs on the lines of the Fiat 500 and of the Maserati, a situation that should end on March 30th. Instead, here's The new frozen shower for bodywork.

1,30 April will stop 1,240 workers of the Fiat 500 electric and one thousand of the Maserati. As mentioned, the motivation adduced from the company is " adapt the production flows of the Fiat 500 BET cars and Maserati GranTurismo to the transitional market trend ".
