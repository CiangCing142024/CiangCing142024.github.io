Our action as MFPR companions and Slai Cobas workers for the SC in the important March 8/Strike of women of this year had two realities at the center in particular.

On the one hand that of the workers, of the workers who live a situation today heavier than loss of jobs, of enormous wage differences, of duties, of denial of even basic rights, of discrimination, of violence even in jobs, to download all the care work and domestic in the family ..., a condition of increasingly exploitation and oppression of the masters, of the government, today with the fascist Meloni, of this social system, but which in the face of all this resist, fight courageous and determinedly , do not stop, or would like to fight ...



On the other that of Palestinian women Which today are the symbol of the most atrocious oppression of the proletarian women of this capitalist and imperialist social system but which transform the immense suffering, the pain, into force, in resistance and struggle.

In the flyers, posters, banners that we brought to the factories of the North, in the jobs, which were in the initiatives of the precarious workers of the Slai, which we gave in the events of Nudm and the Palestinians in which we participated in Milan, we have brought a clear message On the bond between women in struggle, the workers in the first place and the Palestinian women

"... both because it is the Meloni government, the masters of this country are those who build weapons above all, who are the accomplices and co -responsible for the genocide in Palestine. These monsters, as our livers of workers use their profits, Their reactionary, racist, repressive plans to prevent the freedom of us women, are the same who want to crush the Palestinian people and women. The struggle of Palestinian women, therefore, is our struggle, helps us against this putrefied system; and The struggle of us working women is also the struggle of Palestinian women, because it weakens our imperialist monsters ... "(from the flyer of 8 March).

Here is all this has been at the center of our action on this March 8 proletarian and internationalist!

*****

First of all, we were at the Montello/Bg factory at the evening shift between 7 and 8 March, We spoke with several workers of all the reasons for the strike of women, of the need to join as workers, also treasured the struggles already made in recent years, because the masters fear the unity of the workers for this always try to divide them with blackmail, With pressure, repression but we must look with confidence at the strength of the struggle of women ... In this sense, Palestinian women are a great example of the oppression that turns into force ...



The beautiful and strong appeal of an Slai Cobas SC worker workers to the other workers and women to strike " Because we women should not be crushed by anyone " has opened the strike of women/workers since the night of March 8th


On the morning of March 8, we went to the Beretta Salumificio factory, in Trezzo, in this factory the workers by majority immigrant, They also live a condition of increasingly exploitation and less rights, undergo discrimination and differences in treatment of masters and garments and in particular if they fight like some Slai cobas scar workers who have however resisted and have fought, But some workers have also undergone serious harassment from a pig chief in the factory, and courageously also made complaints about it. .. spreading the flyers of the strike and speaking to the workers with interest to the megaphone at the duty a interim immigrant worker in particular wanted to express the sharing of the reasons for the strike and the importance of the unity of workers ". .. alone we are not strength and also fear of losing the job ... " Thus also the rightness of solidarity actions to Palestinian women and to stop the massacre of children and the genocide ...




In the same morning, other flying and supporting actions to workers were made for example in Milan to the national tumor tumors to the nurses and OSS, while in other cities simultaneously in particular in the South such as Palermo and precarious workers of the social coops, of the Nurse, ATA of the Slai Cobas school for the SC and Student on strike were the protagonists of combative garrisons, and the connection, even telephone, of these initiatives to struggle with the actions of the companions among the workers to the factories of the North, was beautiful.
