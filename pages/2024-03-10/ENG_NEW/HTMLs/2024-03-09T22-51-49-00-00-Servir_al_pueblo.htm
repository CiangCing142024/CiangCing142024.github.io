09/03/2024

According to readers of our newspaper, a massive demonstration was held in the city of Albacete where thousands of people participated. A contingent of revolutionaries participated in the demonstration forming a red block, from which they launched strong slogans as "In the fields and in the neighborhoods, proletarian feminism!", "Up the flags of the working woman!" Or "From Gaza to Senegal, the working woman will overcome!"

The revolutionaries marched under flags of the International Communist League (LCI) in Spanish and Arabic, and with the image of Comrade Norah. They distributed hundreds of octavillas by calling the politicization, organization and mobilization of proletarian women. They also inform us that many copies of the number 10 of the newspaper were distributed to serve the people for March-April of 2024.

We share the images we have received in our email.
