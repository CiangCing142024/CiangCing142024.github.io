Gaza – MEMO . The Palestinian commission for the detainees of the prisoners said Monday to fear for the life of the important leader Marwan Barghouti, which was put in isolation in the Israeli prison of Ramla for the third time since the state of employment began his devastating war against the strip of Besieged gaza.

As a high officer of Fatah, Barghouti, 65, was arrested in 2002 and sentenced to four life sentences on charges of having committed hostile acts against Israel during the Second Intifada . The polls indicate that a large slice of Palestinians wants Barghouti to happen to Mahmoud Abbas as president.

The National Security Minister of the Israeli of the far right Itamar Ben-Gvir confirmed last Wednesday that Barghouti is in "isolation". The move, he declared on X, took place after having received "information about a planned ICIFADA [revolt].

“Prevent lawyers from visiting [Barghouti] raises real fears for his life, above all because insulation is accompanied by direct and continuous incitements against him by the media Israelis, "said Qaddoura Fares, head of the commission. "The isolation of Barghouti is part of the aim of the target of all prisoners [...] and leaders of the national movement in captivity, through massive transfer operations, detention collective and in isolation, in addition to systematic torture ".

According to Fares, Ben-Gvir's declaration was “another attempt to show off in front of public opinion Israeli ".

It is the third time that Barghouti is put in isolation within two months.

Before he had been transferred from the prison of Ofer to the isolation of the prison of Ramla and then to the Rimonim insulation unit, before be brought back to Ramla.

Fares asked international institutions and authorities premises to take on their responsibilities and to accelerate the work visiting the prisons and to stop all violations against the prisoners.

The state of the Apartheid has launched a fatal offensive on the Gaza strip following a cross -border incursion led by Hamas on October 7, during the which 1,200 Israeli soldiers and civil soldiers were killed, many of the such as from Israeli tanks and an attack helicopter. The subsequent Israeli bombing killed more than 29 thousand Palestinians And he injured almost 70 thousand. About 60% of civil infrastructures of Gaza has been destroyed and there is a serious lack of first assets necessity, including food, water, medicines and shelters.

Israel is accused of genocide by the International Court of justice. In January, a temporary sentence ordered Tel Aviv by stop the acts of genocide and to take measures to guarantee Humanitarian assistance to Gaza civilians. This measure was widely ignored by the state of employment.
