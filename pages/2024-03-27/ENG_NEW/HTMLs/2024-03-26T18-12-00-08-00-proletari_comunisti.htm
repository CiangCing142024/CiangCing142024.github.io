The Pioltello school challenges Minister Valditara. Just and necessary

of F. Q. | 25 Marzo 2024

The Comprehensive Institute Iqbal still Of Pioltello will remain closed on April 10 on the occasion of the party for the end of the Ramadan . The Council of Institute , con vote unanimous , after reviewing the resolution for the suspension of lessons, declared irregular by the Regional School Office of the Lombardy . The modification concerned the underlining of the alone teaching motivation At the basis of the decision, linked to the numerous predictable absences , defined incomplete in the first drafting from the same minister to education Giuseppe Valditara . The head teacher Alessandro Fansfoni I had announced that he would not bend, passing again from maximum collegiate organ of the school .

Around 7pm the I recommend teachers of the Comprehensive Institute that welcomes approx 1,300 students , between elementary and middle school, voted first, then the approval of the institute council also. The first resolution object of dispute up to the highest levels, It had been voted in May last year. When the news is became public following the declaration of the Eurodeputita league Silvia Sardone , the case has become the object of lit political exchanges. Also the three parish priests of Pioltello, two days ago, with a letter they called “legitimate and consistent with The characteristics of the territorial reality "the choice of school.

However, the inspectors sent by the Ministry had found in the resolution of Technical defects , among which the absence of a teaching motivation , what about it he would have justified the validity. To the Headmaster, from one week targeted by insults and threats, it was therefore asked to “evaluate the disapplication of the resolution and the possibility of the cancellation ". Now the institute council therefore has filled those "gaps", in fact by responding to the sender the disputes.

The Dean had in fact decided the closure, then 'canceled' by the USR and now back, also in consideration of the numerous absences of students in recent years. The school office, in a short note, he spoke of " irregularity " in the resolution approved by the Institute Council and asked to “Evaluate the disappointment of the resolution and the Possibility of cancellation " . The point central had always remained the same: on the one hand there is the autonomy of the Institute to manage school days, on the other there is the center -right - until you even conquer the priority agenda of president of the Senate Ignazio La Russa - which claims that A principal cannot add holidays to the school calendar. “Otherwise there would be The feast of Hindu , of these, of those "to put it in the words of the Second charge of the state.

Fanfoni had already explained to ilfattoquotidiano.it: “We have classes where in recent years on the occasion of the end of Ramadan, of done, they came to school in three or four. THE Children of faith Islamic I'm there majority and despite the guidelines on inclusion consigo to form classi with no more than 30% foreigners , we arrive at 43% because this is our users ”. And therefore he had underlined that he cannot “close his eyes in front of these numbers and reality "and he had hoped that" nobody will come in mind to politicize this decision taken by the Council of the Institute by anticipating the beginning of the Lessons To guarantee all the same rights ".
