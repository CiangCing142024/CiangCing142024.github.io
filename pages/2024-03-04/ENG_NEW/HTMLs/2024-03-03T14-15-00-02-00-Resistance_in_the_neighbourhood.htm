With good mass and pulse, on Saturday, March 2nd, the course of the defense of the popular residence, and the complaint of the policy and auctions policy in the neighborhood of Kallithea, where the "Dovalue" headquarters are housed. The mobilization began with a gathering and microphone at ISAP station, and continued with a march in central places in the neighborhood, as well as the company's offices.

The presence of the repressive forces in the neighborhood has been continuous and provocative during it, which can only be interpreted as another example of the intentions of the system to suppress any efforts to resist and claim.

In the mobilization they called for initiatives against auctions, neighborhood shapes and organizations/collectives. The roads of resistance, intervening with distinct banners, announcement and slogans, emphasized the necessity of connecting the struggle against the evictions with the struggle to defend the popular home before it "passes" into the hands of the various banks/funds/services, as well as the necessity The struggle for the defense of the popular housing with the struggle for health - EDUCATION - WORK AND SUBJECTIVE RIGHTS. Positive was the partition of the world of Kallithea where not only did he take the resistance roads but also congratulated!
