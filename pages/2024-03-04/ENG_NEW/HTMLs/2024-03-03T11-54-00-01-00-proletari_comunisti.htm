Strike in Mirafiori. It is the answer unitary, which has not been seen for at least 15 years, of five Union acronyms, Fiom, Fim, Uilm, Fismic, Ugl, Aqfc, in protest against The car crisis in Turin. The Shareholders' Meeting of Stellantis delegates. reunited today at the Teatro Q77 in Turin has decided to proclaim an 8 -hour provincial strike in April with manifestation of workers in the automotive sector (Stellantis e induced), to request the safeguarding and relaunch of the sector. The former Fiat plant is in layoffs until March 30th.

Unitary mobilization

There mobilization will be organized with the aim of raising awareness local institutions, the government and the

Automotive industrial system on deep crisis that is going through the car sector in Turin . "The automotive table and those of crisis at Mimit have not yet given adequate answers," says the unions.

Organizations ask for a concrete commitment to be brought New productions in Turin and so that the entire sector is supported to deal with the employment reinforcements of the transition process.

Over the next few weeks, Workers' Assemblies provided for in all companies in the sector Automotive. At the provincial event of April is the presence of national secretaries is planned.

Automotive and induced

«Turin He is going through a difficult moment. The automotive with the induced is In the middle of an unprecedented crisis - explains Luigi Paone, secretary General Uilm of Turin -. At this historical phase, the unions Metalmeccanici have chosen to take a unitary path for a common goal: Re -launch the Italian Automotive in the territory in which this was born. For these reasons, 15 years after the last unitary initiative, workers and metalworking workers yes they are gathered and have decided to strike and manifest, to bring New models in Mirafiori and relaunch the induced ».
