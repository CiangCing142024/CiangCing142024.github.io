Thessaloniki organizations and colleges, with the participation of members of the Palestinian community, are organizing a screening of Abby Martin's documentary, "Gaza Fights for Freedom" (2019).

We invite the Thessaloniki to watch in the first Greek translation a shocking documentary that highlights the fair struggle of the heroic people of Palestine for a lighter and independent homeland. We are struggling to cease the slaughter of the Palestinians in the martyred Gaza, to cease all Greece's involvement in Israel-US/NATO murder plans.

We are waiting for you on Saturday, March 9, at 7:30 pm at the Thessaloniki Labor Center to watch this shocking documentary together.

Voluntary reinforcement of 3 € upon entry.

For the documentary (summary from Peoplesforum.org):

This first feature film by journalist Abby Martin began while reporting in Palestine, where the Israeli government banned her to enter Gaza on charges of being a "propagandist". Thus, Abby was associated with a group of journalists in Gaza to produce the film through the excluded border. It is a documentary on the historical demonstrations of the long way of return, which took place every week from March 2018 to December 2019, but it covers much more. It tells Gaza's story in the past and present, projecting rare archive material that explains the story that has never been recognized by the media. You hear from the victims of the ongoing massacre, including journalists, doctors and the family of internationally recognized paramedic Razan al-Najar. At its core, "Gaza Fights for Freedom" is a detailed Israeli army indictment for war crimes with exclusive documents and a stunning cinematic depiction of the Palestinian heroic resistance.
