By a commentator for the Earn Folket Media.

Our comrades in Socialist Revolution report that the Danish war industry has sold material to the Israeli army actively used in their folk murderer campaign against the Palestinians in Gaza.

They write that the war manufacturer "Mydefence" in November and December in Fjord sold anti -rone equipment worth tens of millions of Danish kroner to Israel. The equipment in question is designed to give individual soldiers warning about incoming drones and the opportunity to "jam" them. Bilt has a great demand for such technology since the Palestinian resistance movement has begun to use hobby drones to attack IDF goals.

This comes on top of the already existing arms trade that Danish imperialism does with Israel, including parts for the F-35 fighter aircraft used in the merciless bombing of Gaza. This is a clear violation of obligations made through The UN's arms trafficking treaties As we have heard from Norwegian war manufacturers, such as Nammo, Danish companies also try to waive the responsibility by claiming that the treaties do not apply to sales to third -partnations such as the United States, knowing that these weapons and parts will be resold to Israel.
