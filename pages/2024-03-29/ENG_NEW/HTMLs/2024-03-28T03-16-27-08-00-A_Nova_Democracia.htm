On March 10, the Popular Popular Defense Committee of Pinhais held a warm celebration of the International Women's Day with women in the region, youth and adults, bringing together more than 50 participants. The event was organized with the support of the local residents' association and was attended by the representative of the Anita Garibaldi Women's Movement (MFAG) and the New Democracy Newspaper Support Committee.

The meeting aimed to celebrate this important date of the international proletariat, recalling its class origin, as well as promoting an important debate on the oppression of women of the people and discussing the claims of the region's population, especially those that affect women.

The event was opened with a warm talk of welcome by the Sanitary Committee, in which the participants, the organizations present, the women of the people from all over the world and especially to Palestinian women and their heroic resistance; The occasion when the countless crimes of war of the Zionist state of Israel with the support of imperialism, especially Yankee, was also denounced.

The Anita Garibaldi Women's Movement presented the organization and performed an enlightening exposure on the origin of March 8, female oppression and the revolutionary path to its overcoming. The opposition to bourgeois feminism, opportunism and electoral farce was also demarcated.

The speech of the representative of the Residents Association rescheduled the importance of all the presence of all and the need for the organization of women of the people in their places of housing, to fight for their rights and hold more meetings, courses and meetings like this.

Following, he continued for a conversation activity in which participants were able to discuss about the overload caused by domestic work, the care of family members and the need to search for sources of income; the numerous cases of domestic violence in the region; The unanimous denunciation of lack of access to day care and health in the region, known for precarious, time consuming care and lack of medical specialists in family health units. Both complaints and demands, and referrals and suggestions for the next meeting were systematized in a mural. A climate of agitation was visible, with the expectation of fighting collectively for the needs pointed out.

After the community snack, he went to an exciting moment of honors. Local leaders, recognized for the persistent work of mobilization of the population and solidarity actions in the region, were honored receiving a certificate issued by the Sanitary Committee and Association of Residents.

To boost the presence of mothers at the event, daycare activity was organized throughout the meeting, which served several children.

The event ended with a cultural presentation of Brazilian popular music and the commitment to hold new meetings in a brief period, in recognition of the need for the permanent organization of women of the people.
