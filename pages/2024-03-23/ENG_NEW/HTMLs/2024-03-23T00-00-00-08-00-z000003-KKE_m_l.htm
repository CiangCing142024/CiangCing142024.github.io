After completing the work of the House of Inquiry Committee on the Crime of Tempi, based on the publicity of the signatures issue and with the recent black anniversary and anger of the world expressed in the strike on 28 February, the climate of the climate. It was burdened for the government and made it difficult for the concealment operation.

The government, as expected, went on the offensive and moved on two main axes: First, it accused the opposition of instrumentalizing the tragedy and using the dead and the pain of their relatives in its political confrontation with it (political leeches, government spokesman Marinakis described them). And secondly, she presented herself as the only one responsible who could really satisfy the demand of the relatives, but also of all the people, for full clarification of the case and punishment of the guilty with real and objective evidence.

Mitsotakis himself moved in these directions in his interview with SKAI TV. Taking the well-known, apparently elaborated by his communication team "human" style, he said that he did not forget the sights and smells the next day, when he went to the scene of the crime, the debris of which was imposed by the human need to support the cranes somewhere to lift the wagons and retrieve the bodies. His humanity was exhausted somewhere there, because he was very cynical when he said that the railway is as safe as it can be, without ruling out, in fact, more deaths in the future...

In fact, the ND turned all over the people. He knows that the crime of Tempi has caused his anger, which has repeatedly expressed on the street, at very massive rallies, but even in the one and a half million signatures, despite the objections we have about them.

What worries her, much more than she wants to show, is that this wrath of the world accumulates and concerns almost all aspects of his life. One of all the issues that opens up daily on the people's and youth rights can become the drop that will make the glass overflow, with unpredictable consequences.

However, pressure is being exerted on the government on the EU side, following the intervention of the European Public Prosecutor's Office and the questioning of parliamentary immunity and the law on ministerial responsibility, which has opened up the whole debate on whether European legislation takes precedence over the relevant Article 86 of the Constitution. Also, after Karystianou's speeches in the European Parliament, it was decided to keep in operation the competent EU committees for the control of the judicial process, which, however, is progressing very quickly, by Greek standards.

The question is where. Pressures by the EU do not have the ... pure intention of the administration of justice to a member state that is suffering in this area. But they are moves on the political-geopolitical chessboard, aiming for a member state that is increasingly bound to the chariot of the great transatlantic ally and suffers in the field of ... dedication to the Union.
