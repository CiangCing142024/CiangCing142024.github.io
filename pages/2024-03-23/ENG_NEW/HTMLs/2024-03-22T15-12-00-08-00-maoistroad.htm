The arrests are related to events that occurred during The state fight against genocide in the Gaza Strip of February 7 and have been free doors with positions.

The ten activists are accused of crimes of disorders public, damage, illicit demonstration and criminal group, which can behave prison sentences.

The ten activists had been arrested in different parts of the Metropolitan Area of Barcelona in an operation coordinated by the General Information Police Station of the Mossos d’Esquadra.

The eight initial arrests occurred on Monday, March 18 and All arrested people spent a first night in the dungeons of the police station of Les Corts. On Tuesday morning they started to take a statement at the police station, but it was not until Wednesday that They were transferred to the City of justice To pass to judicial provision. The remaining two people were arrested to noon and on Tuesday afternoon, respectively.

The ten solidarity people are accused of crimes of disorders public, damage, illicit demonstration and criminal group, which can behave prison sentences. To demand their liberation, they were convened Actions on Tuesday afternoon against the police station and Wednesday for the Tomorrow in the City of justice . The calls received support from different solidarity groups with Palestine and antirrepressants, including AGIDA - Anarchist collective defense.

Activists participated in the fighting day of the last February 7 convened with the motto “We estimate life, we defend To Palestine, we stop the world ”in which thousands of people mobilized throughout Catalonia and other points of the state coinciding with the four months of the start of the new genocidal offensive of the state of Israel against the Palestinian people. On that day, in which the CGT and IAC convened partial strike in Catalonia, it was demanded massive the rupture of economic relations and institutional with the state of Israel. In mobilizations, Indra, Carrefour, Comsa, Axa and McDonald’s companies were indicated as "Accomplices" of violence against the Palestinian population.
