In the Very central via Indipendenza of Bologna, the police today loaded and Pushed university students for days in mobilization.

Yesterday In Bologna, university students had protested in front of the Academic Senate to ask for the end of the censorship against those who mobilize against the war in Palestine, to demand the boycott of agreements with Israel and the military sector industrial.

The Academic Senate refused to terminate the agreements in the field military and war, expressing himself with yet another vaga formula e smoky, substantially reiterating the need to respect the regulations and the ethical code which, as regards the dual use, yes I limit to "minimizing any damage" ...

“ We want And we demand a concrete commitment of the University that goes to terminate All agreements with the Leonardo group, with NATO and with the partners of the Zionist government of Israel ”wrote the Students to change the course of the Bolognese university.

“ The mobilizations in our country show that getting the boycott is possible and we can do it only through the struggle, as happened yesterday to the academic Senate of the University of Turin, where thanks to the mobilization has been able to obtain non -participation of the Torinese University to the new Maeci of cooperation tender between Italy and Israel".

But Today Minister Bernini arrived in Bologna for the inauguration of the academic year and the students mobilized to do hear, also in light of the Minister's statements against Students and teachers of the University of Turin who have decided to interrupt the collaboration with the Israeli institutions.

The procession of the students who arrived in via Indipendenza tried to reach the headquarters of the Manzoni Theater where the ceremony took place with Minister Bernini, but found a deployment against him of the police who soon went to the de facto roads by loading e pushing the students. The procession reappeared the files and is directed in a university area.

There protest was also revealed within the theater where it was present A group of students with the Palestine flag. Francesca representative of the Student Council, intervened shortly before of the intervention of the Minister of the University, Anna Maria Bernini, with Kefiah around the neck. “For months we have been witnessing the systemic censorship of voices from the Palestinian community and what are the requests of student mobilizations ”denounced the Student.
