In the long history of struggle of our popular democratic movement, the March 23 has a particular revolutionary value in everyone's heart how many are fighting for a right and at the same time company they resolutely despise imperialism. On 23 March the companions Bhagat Singh, SukDev and Rajguru have been hanged by power British colonial to crush the liberation movement anti-colonial of that time. The victory of the Bolshevik Revolution he had influenced these three Indian revolutionaries who formed of HSRA (Hindustan's socialist revolutionary association). The Comrade Baghat Singh played a fundamental role in training of the HSRA, and clearly stated that whatever the color dominant classes, whether they are white, black or other skin, it must be continue the fight against all exploiters.

Those same exploiters colluded with the imperialists Today celebrate their martyrdom and in particular the management of the Arss celebrates it who opposed it hard to Bhagat Singh and his cause. The BJP, Political organ of the Arss, today claims all the fighters for the freedom, including the companions Bhagat Singh and his own, have no one moral law even to talk about fighters for freedom e to distort their movement. Our party, PCI (Maoist), makes them their homage revolutionary and fights for the realization of their revolutionary aspirations.





The Central Committee of the PCI (Maoist) appeals to all democrats, patriots, workers, peasants, dalits and women's organizations, of the Adivasi, of students, writers, teachers, lawyers and Human rights activists to celebrate March 23 with spirit Antimperialist and revolutionary enthusiasm. Appeals to keep seminars, meetings, events supporting the ideas of Comrades Bhagat Singh, Sukdev and Rajguru to report the subtle attempts of the Hindutva forces to saffron Bhagat Singh and i his companions.
