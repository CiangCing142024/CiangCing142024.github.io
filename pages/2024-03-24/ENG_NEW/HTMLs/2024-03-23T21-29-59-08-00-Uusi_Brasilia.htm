Approximately 100 farmers organized by the Poor Peasant Association Jaqueira (Pernambuco) from various communities took back their water source, which had been fenced by a large farmer company.

March 18, about 100 LCP farmer conquerors in Jaqueira, Pernambuco (PE) communities (Barro Branco, Furnas, Cabugi, Monteir, Caiana, Morcego, Caixa d'Andua, Tenório and Sítio Grande) combined their power He surrounded the large farming company Agropecuária Mata Sul S/A, owned by the farm of the farm Guilherme Maranhão.

Since 2015, when the robbery of countries and water sources began, the peasants have lived and produced the former Frei Caneca farm. Former Government Maranhãp, supported by former governor Paulo Camara, set up gun groups that threaten the entire population to expand cattle breeding to international export through the slaughterhouse.

Despite comprehensive evidence of water poisoning by the pest of the water (in addition to the pollution of cattle and the pollution), the Pernambuco Public Ministry (MPPE) and the Pernambuco Environmental Secretariat (CPRH) never began any criminal investigation by Agroopocuárin Mata S/A.

Thus, at the People's Assembly, the peasants decided to implement a large act to take the water sources back from the large farm. The invitation reached the entire local population in flying markets and in all peasant communities affected by this environmental crime and against the health of the people. The peasants also organized joint efforts to clean and isolated water sources, 50 meters from the livestock farmer and eucalyptus plantations.

To do all this work, the status advocates dropped José Ricardo's revolutionary area of peasants and young students in the Mangue Vermelho movement. They helped to clear the bushes and mark fences to protect water sources in the scorching sun. More, they met alongside the rest of the people, fighting the provocations of the gentlemen.

As soon as the peasants had completed their joint effort with the first water source of the day, singing folk songs and shouting slogans, half -time rifle from large farms appeared with tractors, stage farms, motorcycles and quads. The gunmen arrived with the posts, thread and eucalyptus seeds to put them in the same area that the peasants had just cleared. When the peasant march faced bands, representatives of the farmland began to threaten the peasants with firearms. The voltage led to a confrontation, three guns were injured, and the droon of the big farm was destroyed by the furious folk of firearms.

This same March 18: A MPPE agricultural promoter, CPRH agents, representatives of the State Committee to monitor agrarian conflicts (CEACA), representatives of the pasture commission (CPT) and Pernambuco workers and peasant federation (Fetape) perhaps A large farming company Agropecuária from Mata Sul S/A visited to study complaints of the peasants made by the farm. According to this information, this Commission did not visit the water sources of the peasants, but only to the places shown in the farm.

However, the powerful action of the peasants forced the entire Commission to introduce the act at the scene of the Aktio and to approve two lawyers of the Brazilian Association of Folk Alliance (Abrapo) and the Commission of the Barro Branco Farm Association, and also had to accept the urgent conditions of the people in the public.

In the end, Mppe approved the proposal for the peasants represented by Abrapo to create a "water corridor" to separate the water sources of the people. The status of the farm was ordered to pay the poles and the thread, the city administration sends employees and it was the responsibility of the State Environmental Department to investigate people's waters to determine pollution and to open a criminal investigation against the farm.

After hearing the court, Guilherme Maranhão's gunmen asked for reinforcements from the Pernambuco Military Police (PM-PE) to plant eucalyptus to one of the water sources, which had been cleaned up by the peasants, and the farm lawyer Eduardo Figueredo proclaimed that they would complete the milestone.

Two vehicles were sent to bring security to the gunmen to continue their anti -folk action. This is not a courage to show, but rather a cowardice in front of a small display of peasants connected and organized by the agrarian revolution. It also demonstrates how dedicated the military forces of this old state are about protecting the interests of the state of space and promoting the poverty of the poor in the country and in the city.

The peasants occupying the former Frei Caneca status are determined to take back all their water sources and their land from the hands of the farm. In this way, they invite all peasants in the southern forest area of the Pernambuco to rise against the rifle groups of large farms and the armed forces of this old state.
