By a commentator for the Earn Folket Media.

After 10 years in captivity, G.N. Saibaba, human rights activist and former university professor, finally comment to the public. The comrade is acquitted and released, despite the fact that the ancient Indian state had sentenced him to a lifetime imprisonment for alleged connections to the Maoists.

A press conference is held, where Saibaba touches and urgently tells why he became an activist, about the problems in India and about the process against him. Many media showed up, and the press conference was published by several of these. All over the world, revolutionary and democratic movements have struggled to defend Saibaba's life, and all of these can now celebrate the release.
