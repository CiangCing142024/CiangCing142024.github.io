The corresponding document is entitled "Bundeswehr of the future". In the document, various points are mentioned that must be executed to make the German army "war -related" again. One point of this is, among other things, the strengthening of the sub -forces. Partial forces are a division of the armed forces into various specialized areas. In Germany, the army comprises three partial forces. Army, Navy and Air Force. In addition, there will also be a fourth partial dispute that affects the area of the "cyber and information room" in the future.

In addition, there should also be restructuring in the leadership of the operations that aim to strengthen centralization. In the future, as the document means “from a single source”. this operational management command are controlled and coordinated centrally.
