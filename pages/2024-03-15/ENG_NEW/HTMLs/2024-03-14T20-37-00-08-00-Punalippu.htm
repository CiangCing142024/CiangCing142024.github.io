Wednesday 13.3. A discussion was held in Helsinki under the title "What is proletarian feminism?".

There was a presentation on the subject, after which there was a lively discussion on the subject. The debate again showed that the troops are interested in proletarian feminism as opposite of bourgeois and small bourgeois feminism, which dominate public debate and the left.

According to the organizers, the mobilization of the troops to the event was successful, and they also consider the opportunity to be otherwise successful.
