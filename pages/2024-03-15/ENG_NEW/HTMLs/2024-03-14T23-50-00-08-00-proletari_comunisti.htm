Outside the police from Diaz ”, This is the banner that appeared on Tuesday morning, and immediately removed, on Cancelli of the Pertini Institute - Diaz in Genoa, the school where in 2001 The police beatings against the G8 demonstrators took place. To hang it, students and students of the Institute to protest against An initiative of work orientation with law enforcement agencies .

The work orientation initiative had been scheduled by teachers as part of the thirty hours of training orientation provided for by law , mandatory from this year, and also hosted a stand to "sponsor" his career in the police.

Students and students reiterated the their clear opposition not only with the banner, but also through a leaflets and its social pages. “A few days later from the violent positions of the police against the students of Pisa, Florence and Naples, we refuse that the police, right in a school like this symbolic like ours, it can come to make lessons, as if nothing if ", they say, adding,“ for the freedom of expression, against the militarization of schools ”.
