Resistance to the north ... for popular problems!

Event - discussion

Saturday 2 March, 12:30

4th Elementary School of Nea Ionia (Patriarch Ioakim 2)

This is the name of our political shape that takes its first steps in our neighborhoods, in Nea Ionia, Nea Heraklion, New Philadelphia and Galatsi ! A name that condenses and is the trigger for the road we need to walk!

Accuracy exhausts popular income! Continuous price, expensive products on supermarket shelves, inflated electricity bills, low wages and unemployment make our lives lifeless. Auctions threaten and take our homes, the hardships of a lifetime. We don't praise! We are fighting for life with dignity and rights!

Students and students are fighting for public and free education! It is all of us a duty to support these mobilizations, against government repression and terrorism. Because the right to studies without class barriers concerns the whole people!

Down the hands from the Health Center of Nea Ionia! They have already kicked out the EOPYY pharmacy, sending it to Agia Paraskevi. Now the Health Center itself is threatened with closure! We are fighting for complete and free care for everyone! Return the pharmacy and fully staff the Health Center!

These are some of the issues we have highlighted with our neighborhood action so far. We are people in the popular struggles, workers, young people, retired, unemployed, who We want to reinforce the struggles for the daily problems that the system's politics puts on the people. In our neighborhoods we want to boost this struggle!

We are constituting a political scheme struggling, open to the people and its participation that will take race initiatives in the neighborhood! In immediate popular and local problems, as well as in general political issues. A place of discussion and organization of our struggle. We are fighting for the creation of outbreaks of resistance in our neighborhoods. For popular organization and initiatives for mobilization on the labor-solid claims! Against the logic of managing jointly with its system and local factors and mechanisms. Against the delusions that elections are changing things for the better.

The rage that fills us with increased accounts, wages - crumbs, unemployment, depreciation of our lives, become a fight power! Only with our own organization, with action, resistance and claiming our rights will we see better days! We have nothing to expect from any Savior except to get the situation in our hands!

We invite you to get to know each other, to find ourselves on the streets of our neighborhoods, to fight together! You will find us in the central squares and roads, outside the ISAP stations, in the popular markets, in the small and large neighborhood struggles.
