Thousands of people framed the concentration that the country's farmers had called in the Constitution today, blocking their tractors in front of the House. The farmers had arrived earlier with the tractors in Athens and headed from Omonia to the Constitution.

In their speeches at the gathering, representatives of blocs from all over Greece have put the demands of their struggle (a survival struggle for the peasantry!), Condemned the government's policy and expressed its continuation.

They also stood in solidarity, through their placements, in the struggle of the students against private universities, condemned the crime in Tempi and also referred to the tragic situation in Thessaly after the floods. They also referred to Euboea demanding firefighting projects. In addition, they called on the strike on 28 February.

At the rally he participated by the KKE (M-L) block, massively distributing his announcement and shouting slogans such as "Farmers-Youth Front, Answer to Mass Mass", "The Blocs to Win-Farmers to Live", " Measures of the Governmental - Victory in Bloc The Rural ”and more. Student racing moves were also bloc. Student associations, workers' unions, leftist and broad organization organizations were also involved that wanted to stand in solidarity in this evolving struggle.


The struggle of farmers must continue decisively, until the satisfaction of their own vital needs, against the deceitful government promises and any pursuit of folding.
