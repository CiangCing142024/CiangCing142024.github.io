From China Pictorial Magazine, March 1971, a brief look illustrated to the Municipality of Paris in its centenary:

On March 18, 1871 the proletariat and other workers in Paris overthrow the reactionary domain of the traitor government of the French bourgeoisie ... and established the Municipality of Paris, the first power proletarian revolutionary politician in the history of humanity. It was one Epocal revolution of great flow for the whole world. Was the first attempt by the proletariat to overturn the bourgeoisie and to establish the dictatorship of the proletariat.

During the Franco-Prussian war, France had undergone one Disastrous defeat. The French bourgeois government, traitor, had transferred territories to the enemy and paid large sums of money for the war allowance, while implementing a cruel repression of the people at home. It is in this situation, in which the entire nation was tormented by a growing misery and class contradictions yes they acute every day, that the proletariat and the other workers of Paris launched an armed insurrection.

After its foundation, the Municipality of Paris adopted a series of measures aimed at abolishing the old permanent army, to build the forces Armies of the people, to completely destroy the old system bureaucratic politician who enslaved the people and to create a system of universal suffrage. It is just as Marx underlined: the Municipality of Paris is "the dawn of the great social revolution that will free Forever humanity from the regime of the classes ".



However, since at the time the French proletariat was not sufficiently mature and powerful and was missing from the guidance of a party Marxist politician, the leaders of the Municipality of Paris were unable to give following the victory of 18 March obtained by chasing and annihilating completely the reactionary forces of Thiers, piled up in Versailles. Did not carry out a firm and decisive repression of the counter -revolutionaries. They did not expropria the capitalist bank of France. Nor managed to form an alliance with the farmers. All of these factors led to the final strangulation of the great proletarian revolution by the joint forces of the enemy in homeland and abroad. But in front of the frenetic counterattacks of the enemy, The heroic sons and daughters of the Municipality of Paris fought valiantly in a spirit of maximum devotion to the revolution. For Defending the Municipality fought to the last drop of blood. Their brave and heroic deeds will always shine with a light dazzling.

At the time of the revolution of the Municipality of Paris, Karl Marx yes He found in London. He actively supported the revolutionary initiative of Parisian proletariat. Two days after the defeat of the Municipality of Paris, Marx, in his brilliant work The civil war in France , described the great achievements obtained by the heroes of the Municipality e he summarized the experience and lessons of this revolution. In light of the attempt by the Municipality of Paris to establish the dictatorship of proletariat, Marx stressed: "La working class cannot take possession purely it's simply of a car state already ready e motorcycle per his proper Fini ".
