View of the documentary "Gaza Fights for Freedom"

Teachers' racing moves and class march call workers and youth in screening the documentary "Gaza Fights for Freedom" on Wednesday, 27/3, at 19:00, at the University of Middle Karlovassi. A creation by American journalist Abby Martin, in collaboration with Palestinian reporters, had been banned from entering the Israeli authorities, which reflects Israel's timeless extermination policy over the Palestinians, referring to the demonstrations of the "big track" Country in Gaza from March 2018 to December 2019. On the occasion of the promotion we seek to open the conversation on the island's teachers and workers on the very crucial issue of Palestine and the need to create conditions for a massive mobilization Solidarity of the struggling Palestinian people and condemnation of the murder state.
