We hereby share an unofficial translation of a report we have received by the Unitary Campaign for the Liberation of Georges Ibrahim Abdallah (GIA).

Hereby is attached the report in pictures of this new international day of action called by the Unitary Campaign and for which, this November 14 – the day before the deliberations – more than 30 cities responded. There were mobilizations in already 18 cities on the mobilizations which took place three weeks earlier, on October 24.

For years, months and weeks, the mobilization has continued to amplify and intensify in a joint and coordinated effort on now regular highlights which blatantly and clearly demonstrate the popular and united momentum which is now at our side to demand the release of our comrade until his actual release.

On this November 14, everywhere, we demanded that the next day, the 15 th , Georges Abdallah be released; it is now done but the struggle continues until the final victory so that Georges Abdallah is free! Let’s continue the mobilization with this same determination and just like him, let’s not give up!

Georges Abdallah must be free!
