On Saturday, March 16, a large demonstration was held in Copenhagen by Stop Annection of Palestine. The demonstration went from Sankt Hans Torv to the Red Square.

Hundreds of protesters walked on the streets to express their support for the Palestinian people's battle against Israel's brutal genocide in Gaza, which will soon last for half a year, with more than 31,000 Palestinians being murdered. Speeches were held, condemning the weapon sales of Danish imperialism and direct financial support for Israel's genocide, as well as the government's hypocrisy and lies.

Hundreds of Palestinian flags were raised in the strong wind of the day. The same was a red banner with the slogans "Living the heroic Palestinian resistance struggle!" And "It is right to revolt!" In Danish and Arabic.

During the demonstration, Mobilized for March 18: International Solidarity Day with political prisoners. We have received a copy of a flyer that was distributed during the demonstration and reproduces it below:

Proletarians in all countries, unite!

International Solidarity Day with political prisoners

It has always been the case that as long as there has been exploitation and oppression, there have also been uprisings by the oppressed. The same is true today in the Heroic Resistance of the peoples in Palestine, the struggle of the poor peasants and indigenous peoples in Latin America, not to forget the luminous People's Wars in Peru, Turkey, India and the Philippines.

At the same time, it has been such that the exploitative classes in the world have concentrated their repression against the voice of the oppressed matches. The leaders are often exposed to the most brutal torture and oppression. With this, the ruling classes want to send the people a signal: If you revolt we will do the same against you!

Therefore, in the 1920s, the Comintern founded Red Help and on March 18 as the International Solidarity Day with political prisoners. We want to resume this tradition and spread the anti -imperialist solidarity with the struggles of the people around the world.

Come along and mark the International Solidarity Day with political prisoners in the People's House. This year we would like to pay special attention to the solidarity campaign with George Ibrahim Abdallah and the people's battles against imperialist mega projects in Mexico.

At the People's House we will keep the café open at 18:00 to 22:00 on Monday, March 18, where you can buy a cheap cup of tea or coffee and hear about the people's matches.
