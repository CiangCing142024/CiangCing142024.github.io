This book that tells the path of a girl in Bagnoli's Italsider, as well as reading pleasantly, is interesting for at least two reasons, which contribute to the clarity of what the factory means and what it means to women.

The class awareness, the transformation "worker", a worker who struggle, of Vincenzina - who is the daughter of a worker of the Italian Ethialisider but had a completely different perspective compared to that of working in the factory that is frightening, which yes HATE - It happens not easily, with contrasts, resistances, contradictions, but, then, especially with the discovery of the hard factory.
Vincenzina discovers that the factory - who killed the father, like many other workers - is also, and often above all, "community", collective, unity. This community is given by suffering, but is primarily given by the discovery of others, the others; A community that is not outside the factory, but that continues to live, above all, for workers even outside the factory.
Exploitation, heavy, harmful working conditions, the fear of risking life like other workers/workers, the protervy of the leaders who care about life human, everything is there, especially in a steel factory like Italsider; But there is gradually the discovery of strength, of the need to rebel, the joy of fighting, to unite, to be in a "family"; The reason for the pride of Vincenzina's father to be a communist.
Then, all of this, the book describes it as it has lived by women, which is different. For workers, the effort is greater, because they have the weight of the family, the concerns that accompany them in the factory; concerns that they find every day outside, by the need, fear, suffering of aborting in then more terrible conditions, to sisters who delude themselves of an easy way to make money, from economic difficulties, to the subjects submissive, linked like a chain to their men, for better or for worse, etc. etc.
But then, there is knowledge, the discovery of solidarity between women inside and outside the factory, friendships that become strong like steel. And therefore, the class community that is transformation, which gives strength, unity, hope. The factory in which to also find an unlawful, not false love.
We conclude with Vincenzina's words: "... how many hells existed in addition to his. The devil never stopped spreading poison, poisoning, transforming lives and people. In the end, Absurd, the factory saved. At the beginning he had hated him because he had taken his father and had sucked in her too, while now He realized that he had been on the wrong side of the world. That place, 'or construction site, united people, made sure that everyone compact the other for dignity and nobility, so everyone became a single being, a The only breath that sent life ahead ... ".
