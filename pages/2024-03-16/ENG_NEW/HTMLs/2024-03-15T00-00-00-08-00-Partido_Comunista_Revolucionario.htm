The imperialist control of drug trafficking

Sin “ money laundering "There is no narconegocio. But what is again clear is that without imperialist control there can be no "drug trafficking", since the bulk of the drug is not consumed in the country. The great imperialist countries, main consumers, are illegally exported.

And when talking about "commercial wars" it must be taken into account that one of the most lucrative shops worldwide is drugs, at the level of weapons, prostitution, etc.

Dr. Horacio Tabares, has historically analyzed the control of drug trafficking imperialism in depth, with peaks such as the opium war in China, the control of India, etc., to this day.

Huge amounts of marijuana come in barges through the Paraguay and Paraná rivers. They are traveled in the surroundings of Rosario. There are those who claim that this transhipment is already done directly from the barges with Paraguayan flag, to the transatlantic ships without touching port.

Its traffic is controlled by powerful posters linked to the highest government levels of Paraguay and the worst commands of Brazil.

The bulk of cocaine that comes from the Bolivian East, is transported by planes or by Route 34 to the ports of Rosario, where it also embarks for Europe and the United States.

The interimperialist struggle for a strategic area

It is difficult to think that the overflow of the fight for narco traffic is not linked to the struggle for the control of a strategic area such as Rosario, by the bands already established and those that can take advantage of the new international conditions.

But what is ridiculous is that the narcomenudeo, narcopolic bands and their death and pain are removed strengthening the imperialist control of our ports and navigable roads.

Particularly the dredging and beaconing of the so -called "hydrovy" implies a great business, but also implies effective control, in the exact and "just in time" place of river navigation and access to ports, indispensable to ensure the drug trafficking

Allowing the entry of the DEA is like turning off a fire with gasoline. Since the DEA is the body that guarantees the control of the drug that enters the US, which is the main world consumer of drugs and narcotics.

And that in the struggle to liquidate the competition they do not control, it is responsible for drug wars throughout the world, which have caused horrors in many countries of America.

The entrance of the DEA and the Yankee military engineers in our rivers and ports is the access of the fox to the chicken coop, it condemns us to continue suffering the evils that accompany drug trafficking and involves us in cruel wars such as those of Mexico, Colombia, growingly Peru, etc

On the contrary, the struggle to ensure the sovereignty of our country, its borders, its ports, etc. It is the first step to stop the bloodbath that Rosario suffers today.
