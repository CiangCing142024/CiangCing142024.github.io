This morning the interrogation of a guarantee of Anan Yaeesh in the district house of Terni took place. The accusatory prospect accepted by the investigating judge of the L'Aquila court is relating to the organization of a terrorist group called "Tulkarem Brigade", operating in the city of Tulkarem in the West Bank, budding of the brigades of the Martyrs of Al-Aqsa.

Anan has released a long and articulated spontaneous declaration by pronouncing a jâ€™ accusa against the exquisitely political character of the investigation. In particular, he told the condition of oppression and violence suffered by the Palestinian people subjected to the Israeli military yoke in the indifference of the international community. He told his story as a young Palestinian, the killing of his girlfriend by the Israeli army as they went to school, the dirty hands of the girl's blood, the attempted murder of which he was the victim in 2006 by Israeli soldiers, The 11 shots of firearm from which he was reached, the horror tried by the mother to the sight of his injury and the consequent stroke from which he was reached by losing forever the cognitive skills, his friends killed by the army.

He claimed to refuse the stigma of the terrorist for himself and for his people, he recalled that all peoples have the right to fight for their freedom against the invading army, compared the Palestinian struggle to that of Italy during resistance, And he stressed that, rather, no western country has ever grown in supporting Ukraine, which has also been armed by the same West. He recalled the horror of the Gaza war and the 30 thousand Palestinian dead of which over two thirds and children, and rhetorically asked who the terrorists are.

He also recalled that the struggle for Palestine is played in the occupied territories and not elsewhere, referring to this to the articles of newspapers that feared the danger of acts of violence in Italy. He concluded by saying that he only wants peace and freedom for their people.
