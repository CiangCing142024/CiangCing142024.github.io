Today, Thursday 14/3 there was a demonstration in the city center against Pierrakakis.

Student clubs, the racing moves, the SSP, the student course and the space were involved with block.

Despite the picture of removing the last week's collective processes at universities, students continue to be on the road. This shows that the educational rallies that have long been established every Thursday and the fight against (now voted) Pierrakakis law must continue.

With banners and slogans, racing moves highlighted this very necessity of continuing the struggle in the direction of overthrowing the law, against the compromise set by the reformist left about the non -implementation/blockage of the law.
