The contract that Giuseppe Borrelli had with the GI Group, an interim agency. It was the latter that had taken it permanently and could move it from one customer to another On the basis of the requests and it was Gi Group who paid him the salary on the basis of the contractual settlement of metalworkers.

The figure of This young worker is of staff leasing, that is, for rent, worker for rent.

To the company this was very well because they had qualified personnel but, in the at the same time, no direct contractual constraints with them.

With the contract Staff Leasing The worker feels perpetually in the balance, lives one condition of eternal precariousness in each of the factories in which Work. If you are a leasing staff worker and you are considered a Rompisciolele, the company takes you and respects you with the sender without have no obligation to keep you at work.

Gianfranco Conte, 38 years old, married and father of two children, died crushed by one coil while working in the Jindal Films factory that it produces Polyethylene movies, therefore a type of production different from that of the steel. He was maneuvering a carroponte inside the factory when it was overwhelmed by a coil, it was centered in full chest. The impact was violent, remained for a few minutes reactive, so much so that he himself turned off the switch of the machine and asked for help. But gradually that the minutes They flowed worse, he lost his senses. Upon arrival of the ambulance The conditions were very serious. The impact with the coil caused internal injuries.
