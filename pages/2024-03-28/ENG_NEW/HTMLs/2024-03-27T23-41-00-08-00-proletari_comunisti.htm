Genova . Against the economic and academic agreements between government, Italian universities, Israel and companies active in the war supply chain, today also in Genoa - such as In many other cities - the protest of the students and of Some professors: 14 The Teachers , some precarious workers who have signed the appeal to the Rector Federico Delfino per interrupt the collaboration paths that they are somehow connected with attacks on the Palestinian population.

Protest started at dawn with a presidio in the atrium of the rectorate, in via Balbi 5, with banners, banquets and an exhibition on the history of the conflict in the Middle East, but then it is overlooked in a real Empress of an autonomous group of students in the room that housed the meeting of the Academic senate , then interrupted. First, for hours, the same students had protested out of the door screaming slogans like "Outside NATO from the University" , playing drums and reading the motivations of the garrison with the megaphone.

boys and girls, on the other hand, have contested openly the rector Federico Delfino both during the Senate and before, during a short meeting within his office . On this occasion Delfino was apostrophized with insults of the type "Genocida, murderer" because, not wanting to agree to suspend the Agreements under accusation, has been defined as an accomplice of those who carry out i bombings on Gaza.


On the spot present officials of the Digos . “After a participated garrison, the Rector Delfino abandoned the senate session, reconfirming No step back Compared to the death agreements, no dialogue with the students ", they declare to change course.

“In the closure of the comparison - they conclude - he then exploited the mobilization to do pass the narrative of the contrast between "good and democratic" students and "bad and violent" students (carefully avoiding talking about the numerous teachers present) for Divide us: we respect these accusations, the academic community to the sender will not be divided by these exploitation, combined alongside the Palestinian resistance ".
