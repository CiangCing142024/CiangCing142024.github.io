Kathmandu, February 18, 2024: The Socialist Front of Nepal, a coalition of four Communist and Socialist parties, is convening a meeting today to review its organizational expansion throughout the country so far and it’s political activities. The meeting is taking place at the CPN (Unified Socialist) office at 9.00 am.

The meeting’s agenda includes an in-depth examination of the government’s performance, a thorough analysis of the current political landscape in the country, and an exploration of international political dynamics, said the CPN (Unified Socialist) Office Secretary Jeevan Ram Shrestha.

Leaders from the CPN (Maoist Centre), Janata Samajwadi Party, CPN (Unified Socialist), and the Communist Party of Nepal led by Netra Bikram Chand are participating in the meeting.

The Socialist Front of Nepal comprises the CPN (Maoist Centre), the Janata Samajwadi Party, the CPN (Unified Socialist) and the Communist Party of Nepal led by Netra Bikram Chand. CPN (Unified Socialist) Chairperson Madhav Kumar Nepal is the current coordinator of the Front.
