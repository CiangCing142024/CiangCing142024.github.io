Turin, Pro Palestine procession and armored synagogue. An image of Meloni with Netanyahu.


A bombing to Gaza is simulated while the children They play soccer. La Russa: "Solidarity with Meloni"

Pro Palestine procession started from Largo Marconi , in San Salvario. After the Thursday afternoon clashes in via Verdi , with 7 wounded and 50 -identified policemen, still accusations for Rai "Censorship tool" and choirs against Israel and the Italian government. In the procession present the flags to change course, power to the people, USB and Rifondazione as well as anarchists and antagonists.

Also in the sights l'Arcardo Iren-Mekorot And the failure to create "real humanitarian corridors for the population of Gaza ».

The procession paralyzed the traffic in via Nizza and is in Piazza Castello The long serpentone that during 3 hours of travel, with considerable inconvenience for Traffic in the center saw the participation of a thousand people. In via Po, a short distance from Rai, protected From the armored carabinieri the new attack on state television, greeted by a burst of whistles: «The Rai director apologized For the words said by Ghali in Sanremo is a symptom of a country that does not accepts dissent ».

In Piazza Castello there was a Flash mob under the balconies of the Prefecture: a bombardment was simulated In Gaza while children play football. A group of demonstrators burned a poster that portrayed the handshake between the premier Giorgia Meloni and the Israeli Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu.

Hundreds in procession for Palestine, slogans and banners: "Israel Assassin"

Behind the banner «against repression and war. Palestine Libera »there are several hundred people who went down to the street a Turin for Palestine. The event, promoted by the coordination For Gaza and the coordination of 2 December, he started from Largo Marconi, a San Salvario. «Against the genocide of the Palestinian people, the enlargement of the conflict in the Middle East and the participation of the Italian government in the military mission in the Red Sea », is written in flyer.
In procession various delegations of the coordination of the Turin mosques, Communist Refoundation, Power to the People, yes Cobas, change course and several boys of the student collectives. «The peoples in revolt In revolt they write the story Intifada until victory "," Israel Assassin "," Meloni Boia "," Netanyahu assassin "the slogans.

Tuesday afternoon at the garrison in via Verdi, in front of the Rai headquarters, promoted by "Palestine Libera Project" some tensions had broken out.

Launch of glass bottles, sticks and stones against the police who they replied with three charges. About fifty people were identified, including anarchists and militants of the Social Center Askatasuna. Seven agents and some demonstrators were injured.


The procession ended with a Flash Mob of complaint. «We Let's do for pretend, but these are scenes that can be seen daily Gaza, "says the speaker. Two children play football while two more They show a sign: "Ride us our childhood". The background is the noise of the bombings: women cry over the bodies of the dead, Others try to recognize them. Doctors and nurses try to help the wounded. Journalists document, a photographer collapses hit.
"In Gaza - says a sign - those who are alive are far from living".
