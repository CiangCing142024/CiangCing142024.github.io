It was successfully held on Friday, February 16 at the Chania Labor Center, the screening of the documentary "Gaza Fights for Freedom" by the Resistance Initiative. A documentary that highlights the timeless struggle of Gaza residents against the inhumane and degrading exclusion of the Zionist state of Israel and the fascist type of their protest, resulting in thousands of dead and injured from 1948 to 2019. . But mainly through the words of the Palestinians themselves, he sends a message of resistance and determination that they will not stop fighting for a lighter homeland.

In a short position by a member of the Resistance Initiative, the need to re-enter our city the anti-imperialist movement and to demonstrate our solidarity in the Palestinian resistance, demanding that the country be not involved in the slaughter of the Palestinian people. In particular, it was emphasized that the unfolding of the anti -imperialist struggle supplies and is fueled by the struggles of farmers and students because they are struggles that oppose the policy of dependence, oppression and war.
