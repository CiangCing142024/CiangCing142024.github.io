There are some more reports on actions celebrating the 130-year anniversary of the birth of Chairman Mao Tse-tung.

In Oslo , Norway, graffiti was made with slogans of the international campaign of the ICL for the Birth of Chairman Mao.

In Leipzig , Germany posters celebrating the 130th anniversary of the birth of Chairman Mao were put up in the working-class district of Leipzig-Schönefeld.
