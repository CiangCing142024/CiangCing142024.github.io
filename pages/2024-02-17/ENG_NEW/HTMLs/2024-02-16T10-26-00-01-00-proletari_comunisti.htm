February 11th


Palestinians lined up to take water in a refugee camp in Gaza.

The numbers can be inhumanizing. Alone, hardly they could transmit the reality of the Israeli genocide in progress to Gaza. The latest official numbers, however, are critical.

The government office for the media of Gaza has published an update On the scope of the destruction made by the Israeli army in the besieged gaza strip, starting from 7 October.

The count is not definitive, due to the fact that over 7,000 Palestinians are dispersed and are presumed to have died - under the rubble of their homes, or scattered through the streets in areas that Civil Protection operators cannot reach.

The numbers, statistics and estimates that concern the period between 7 October 2023 and 11 February 2024.

The genocide in numbers

- 128 days of war.
- 2,438 massacres.
- 35,176 martyrs and missing.
- 28,176 martyrs whose bodies have reached hospitals.
- 12,300 martyred children.
- 8,400 martyrs.
- 340 martyred health workers.
- 46 martyrs of the Civil Protection.
- 124 Martyrs journalists.
- 7,000 missing; 70% of them are children and women.
- 67,784 injured.
- 11,000 injured need to travel to receive critical and life -saving treatments.
- 10,000 oncological patients at risk of death.
- 700,000 Gazese infected with infectious diseases due to the displacement.
- 8,000 cases of viral hepatitis infection due to the displacement.
- 60,000 pregnant women are at risk due to the lack of access to health care.
- 350,000 chronic patients are at risk due to the lack of administration of drugs.
- 99 arrests of healthcare professionals.
- 10 arrests of journalists whose name is known.
- 2 million displaced people in the Gaza strip.
- 142 government offices destroyed by employment.
- 100 schools and universities completely destroyed by employment.
- 295 schools and universities partially destroyed by employment.
- 184 mosques completely destroyed by employment.
- 266 mosques partially destroyed by employment.
- 3 churches targeted and destroyed by employment.
- 70,000 housing units completely destroyed by employment.
- 290,000 residential units partially destroyed by employment.
- 66,000 tons of explosives launched by employment on Gaza.
- 30 hospitals put out of service by employment.
- 53 health centers put out of service by employment.
- 150 health centers partially destroyed by employment.
- 123 ambulances completely destroyed by employment.
- 200 archaeological sites and assets destroyed by employment.
