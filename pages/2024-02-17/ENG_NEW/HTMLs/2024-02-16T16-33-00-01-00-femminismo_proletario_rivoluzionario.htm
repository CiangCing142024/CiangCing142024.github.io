From Tuesday 6 February at the Turin prison in the women's section a new hunger strike began.
The girls of Turin have made it known, through a letter, the intention of embarking on a relay strike to the relay to draw attention to the prison emergency. In particular, the issues raised concern overcrowding and therefore the need to use more alternative measures, early liberation, hardly granted even in cases of good conduct. An initiative born in support of Rita Bernardini and Roberto Giachetti to be able to constitute a network that from inside and outside the prison walls pressure to obtain these results and that is added to other similar initiatives already carried out by the girls of Turin in recent months.
We reached Stefania to our microphones, recently left the Turin prison and which took part in this protest, which told us the prison conditions highlighting the paradoxes of this institution, its complete uselessness with a view to "reintegration "In society, the blindness of those who organize and govern this place in the face of the demands of the detained population that are the minimum that should be stretched.
《 Dignity is to live in a country where prisons are not overcrowded and the social reintegration of prisoners is guaranteed that even the best guarantee of security》-Mattarella, President of the Republic February 2022

We are the prisoners of the Turin prison, we write this letter because we ask that a deflationary measure is urgently launched: We are 60,000 (it is more) crammed into 47,000 regulatory seats, We would like to support the non-violent initiative of Rita Bernardini and Roberto Giachetti from here to give impulse to the proposed law of modification of the special and ordinance advance liberation! Our voice beyond the bars to recall everyone's attention !!!
The solution is no longer repression or social control, the solution is not the punishment, but to report the legitimacy in this - not a place, where "the same" itself is in the act of crime since the essential rights of the human being are not respected .
