Vienna | 06.03.2024 | Vienna called on the 53rd General Assembly of the Turkish Workers Association on March 10th.
VTID, the first organization of Turkey and Turkey Kurdistani in Austria, is a mass organization organized in the late 1960s.
Organized by students and immigrant workers in the late 60s, VTID has a history of struggle more than half a century and has an important place in the struggle for immigration in Austria.
The call of the VTID Board of Directors is as follows:
”Dear members, fans and friends.
On Sunday, March 10, the date of struggle is approximately as old and glorious as the date of migration in Austria, the VTID Association is held 53rd General Assembly.
We invite all members, friends, friends and veterans to our General Assembly. ”
