Within the international call made by the Communist Party of India (Maoist) for February 23 and 24, the Communist Workers Union (MLM) was proposed to carry out an action in support of Palestine.

In the case of Colombia, for these dates, the existing Palestinian support committees were inviting some activities. Such is the case of the event Together with Palestine In the different cities of the country, in Bogotá a cultural event was held with a concert that sought to gather some artists around the Palestinian cause and by the high genocide. We decided then, unite this activity independently.

The concert that had been announced by social networks and even through some radio stations, managed to gather some artists of different styles and nationalities; On the platform the audience could see rock bands, poets, rappers, traditional Arab music, assemblies, improvisations and folklore.

The activity that was raised by the UOC (MLM) pointed to independent participation with political agitation on support for Palestine trying to unite some forces of the revolutionary workers of some unions and working together with Colombian Support Committee to Palestine . It is cited through social networks to an agitation of flags and banks were extended from Palestine and supporting the Popular War in India, a post of publications was installed and slogans were distributed that were stirred during the development of the concert.

We managed to gather efforts in a symbolic act that gathered us at 4:30 in the afternoon in a busy corner near Bolívar Square, to make from there a small but significant march with banners, flags, megaphones and slogans, we amplify the message of support of the Palestinian people and condemn the genocide committed by Israel with the direct support of imperialism, telling the unsuspecting passersby the destruction to which Gaza is subjected, but also their resistance and international support.

He also participated in the activities carried out in the city of Medellín and Bucaramanga, carrying the propaganda and participating in the complaint against the Zionist State of Israel and the imperialists who sponsor it.

This simple but valuable propaganda activity in the middle of the day of the 23F was carried out with proletarian effort, planning and internationalism, so we give the movement a part of the victory when complying with the planned and the proposed to contribute from this corner of the planet to the planet to the Palestinian cause and proletarian solidarity.
